2. How Motor claims usually work

Lodge your claim online - this can take 15 or more minutes depending on complexity. The more information you provide upfront, the quicker the claim will progress.

We will send you a claim number via sms and email. This will include next steps relevant to you to act on immediately.

We will confirm your claim within three business days, and depending on the complexity may get in touch to clarify details or request particular information.

Repairers may be required to assess the damage to your vehicle. After we receive futher clarification or documentation, this will be added to the claim.

If we need to take particular action from our end, we will update you as soon as this progresses. Complex claims may take longer to resolve.

Once adequate information is available to resolove your claim, you will receive applicable next steps.

If approved, excess and payouts will be finalised.
