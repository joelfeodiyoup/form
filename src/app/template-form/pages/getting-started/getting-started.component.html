2. How Motor claims usually work

Lodge your claim online - this can take 15 or more minutes depending on complexity. The more information you provide upfront, the quicker the claim will progress.

We will send you a claim number via sms and email. This will include next steps relevant to you to act on immediately.

We will confirm your claim within three business days, and depending on the complexity may get in touch to clarify details or request particular information.

Repairers may be required to assess the damage to your vehicle. After we receive futher clarification or documentation, this will be added to the claim.

If we need to take particular action from our end, we will update you as soon as this progresses. Complex claims may take longer to resolve.

Once adequate information is available to resolove your claim, you will receive applicable next steps.

If approved, excess and payouts will be finalised.

3. Have you got a police report?

Police report number

This is mandatory for incidents involving theft, malicious damage, collision and arson. This can be sent after you have lodged your claim if you don’t have this yet

Police lost property report number

This is mandatory for items that are accidentally lost. This can be sent after you have lodged your claim if you don’t have this yet

4. Before you start, collect the following documentation to email through after you lodge your claim

Photos related to incident damage

Such as vehicle, building or item damage

Repair or replacement receipts and quotes

For repair or replacement of anything you have already done related to this claim

Proof of ownership documents for items you are claiming

Such as original purchase receipts or photos of a particular item being used in the past

Other documentation relating to the claim

Anything that could help us assess the claim such as a report or witness account for example

5. How to save and continue your claim online

You can save your claim progress anytime, using the ‘save to MyAccount’ button on each page.

Your draft claim is saved to My Account. If you exit and come back later, log in to My Account and select ‘continue claim’.

Your claim progress will also be automatically saved as you go to make sure you don’t lose important information.

You can cancel your claim lodgement at any stage if you need.

You can print any page you need through this lodgement.

To edit your information, please use the previous or next buttons. Do not use the browser back button.
